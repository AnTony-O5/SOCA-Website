About Us



Founded in Spring 2024 by Briana Date, The Students of Caribbean Ancestry was created to provide a space where students can confidently express their culture while building a strong and lasting community. SOCA is dedicated to celebrating the rich diversity of Caribbean heritage through a variety of engaging events and experiences. From dance workshops and cultural showcases to social gatherings and game nights, we strive to bring students together in a welcoming and vibrant environment. Whether you're looking to connect with your roots or learn more about Caribbean culture, SOCA offers a place for everyone to feel at home.

We invite you to join us and be part of a community that celebrates culture, unity, and joy!
